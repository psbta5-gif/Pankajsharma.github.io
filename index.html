01 — About

Pankaj is an Assistant Professor at Lovely Professional University, Jalandhar (April 2024 – present), where he teaches Digital Marketing for Tourism, Tourism Marketing, Hotel Operations, and Travel Agency Management at UG & PG levels. He designs outcome-based curricula with CO-PO mapping aligned to UGC guidelines and mentors student research.

Before LPU, he spent five years as Senior Research Fellow & Guest Faculty at Himachal Pradesh University, Shimla (2019–2024), teaching PG MTTM/BTTM courses and conducting doctoral research on mountaineering and trekking tourism in the tribal districts of Himachal Pradesh.

He is now extending that research into a five-paper series on climate stability as a destination-competitiveness pillar, while building a second track in business analytics — SQL, Power BI, and data storytelling — toward teaching roles in that space.
